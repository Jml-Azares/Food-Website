The Best Lugaw in Town!

Juana Lugaw

In 2019, prior to the pandemic, Juana Lugaw was founded with the goal of providing students and faculty with the most affordable, delicious, and high-quality lugaw. Unfortunately, due to the pandemic in 2020, the establishment had to shut down. However, in 2022, Juana Lugaw reopened its doors in a new location right in front of the prestigious Mindanao Institute. With its strategic location, Juana Lugaw aims to cater to the basic needs of the students and faculty by providing them with warm and satisfying meals at an affordable price. Whether it's for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, Juana Lugaw is committed to offering its customers a delicious and fulfilling dining experience.
