The Best Lugaw in Town!

Juana Lugaw

In 2019, prior to the pandemic, Juana Lugaw was founded with the goal of providing students and faculty with the most affordable, delicious, and high-quality lugaw. Unfortunately, due to the pandemic in 2020, the establishment had to shut down. However, in 2022, Juana Lugaw reopened its doors in a new location right in front of the prestigious Mindanao Institute. With its strategic location, Juana Lugaw aims to cater to the basic needs of the students and faculty by providing them with warm and satisfying meals at an affordable price. Whether it's for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, Juana Lugaw is committed to offering its customers a delicious and fulfilling dining experience.

Healthy Lugaw from Juana Lugaw

Our lugaw is made with high-quality rice, which is a great source of carbohydrates and provides long-lasting energy. Additionally, our lugaw is cooked to the perfect consistency, resulting in a creamy texture that is easy to digest and gentle on the stomach. But the health benefits of our lugaw don't stop there. Our dishes are also loaded with a variety of toppings that are not only delicious, but also nutritious. Add some shredded chicken or beef strips for a boost of protein, or opt for our assorted seafood or vegetable options for a healthy dose of vitamins and minerals. And for those watching their calorie intake, our plain lugaw dish is a great option, coming in at only 120 calories per serving. Plus, with our affordable prices, you can enjoy a filling and healthy meal without breaking the bank. But don't just take our word for it – come visit us at Juana Lugaw and see for yourself why our lugaw dishes are a great choice for your health and well-being. Whether you're looking for a quick and nutritious lunch, a satisfying breakfast, or a cozy dinner on a chilly evening, we've got you covered. So next time you're in the mood for a healthy and delicious meal, come on down to Juana Lugaw and enjoy the many benefits of our lugaw dishes! We also use stevia, a natural sweetener, in our dishes, including our desserts, which allows us to create delicious and satisfying treats without the added sugar. This makes our desserts a guilt-free indulgence that you can enjoy without compromising your health goals.
